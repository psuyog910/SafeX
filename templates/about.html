About Password Manager

Password Manager is a secure application designed to help you store and manage your passwords safely.

How it works

Password Manager uses strong encryption to protect your passwords. We do not store your passkey, we just store your encrypted passwords.

  • Your passwords are encrypted with your personal passkey
  • Only you can access them with your account credentials

Security Features

  • Strong password hashing
  • Symmetric encryption for stored passwords
  • Protection against unauthorized access

Tips

  • Use a strong, unique passkey for each password
  • Remember your passkeys - they cannot be recovered if lost
